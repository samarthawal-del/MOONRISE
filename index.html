Est. 2024 · Pune

The Beginning

A Dream Born at Midnight

MOONRISE began as a late-night conversation between two lifelong friends — a chef and an architect — who believed Pune deserved a dining experience as beautiful as the city itself after dark.

In 2024, after three years of planning, sourcing, and dreaming, we opened our doors at 12 Moonlight Avenue. Sold out every weekend for our first three months.

Our philosophy is simple: every guest deserves to feel like the evening was made for them. The moon doesn't hurry. Neither do we.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2021

The Idea is Born

Over a late-night dinner in Paris, Aryan and Meera sketch the first concept of MOONRISE on a napkin.

2022

Building the Team

Aryan returns to India. Meera begins designing the space. The founding team assembles.

2023

The Space Takes Form

12 Moonlight Avenue is transformed over 14 months through Meera's architectural vision.

Jan 2024

Grand Opening

MOONRISE opens. Sold out for the first three months. Featured in Times Food and Condé Nast Traveller India.

2024 — Present

Growing, Glowing

Over 10,000 guests hosted. Seasonal menus launched. Jazz nights introduced. Private dining suite coming soon.
